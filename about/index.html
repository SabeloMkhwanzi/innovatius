Our history

Innovatius Consultants are specialist consultants in the field of Occupational Health, Safety Environmental Management. We focus on providing SHEQ services through consultancy and mentorship of small, medium, and large business, construction projects, manufacturing and corporate.

Innovatius Consultants are also 100% Black Owned Level 1 (135% B-BBEE procurement recognition) We have a dedicated professional team with over 18 Years of experience who utilise their expertise and knowledge to assist clients in achieving a safe and compliant workplace, a team with experience in risk management who provide a practical approach to safety system implementation.

We offer business safety for higher reward and give a complete feasibility and effective level of compliance, standards, safety performance and safety management strategies.
